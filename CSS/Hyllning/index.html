Markiplier

Mark Edward Fischbach

Mark Edward Fischbach, known online as Markiplier, is an American YouTuber, gamer-commentator, actor, and comedian. Originally from Honolulu, Hawaii, he began his career in Cincinnati, Ohio, and is currently based in Los Angeles, California.

As of July 2020, his channel has over 27 million subscribers, over 14 billion total video views, and is the 48th most-subscribed channel on YouTube. Fischbach specializes in Let's Play videos, commonly of survival horror video games.

He has also given away a lot of money to charity, such as Cincinnati Children's Hospital Medical and Center and Best Friends Animal Society
